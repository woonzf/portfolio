How I started

During my time as Operation Executive, I had the chance to make excel formulas such as BOM generator and machine uptime calculator which eliminate the time needed to calculate them manually. I enjoyed making and often spent time refining them. It is when I realized I would really like to have a job that allows me to solve problems through programming. And so I decided to look for online programming courses. I found Harvard's CS50x and took the course. I have some experience in programming with C and Matlab during my time in the university so I finished CS50x quite quickly. Then I looked into web development, found and started The Odin Project. Since then, I have completed multiple projects. You can check some of them out down the page or on my GitHub!
